1966 - World Cup winners

In 1966, England won the World Cup on home soil beating West Germany 4-2 after extra-time. To this day Geoff Hurst remains the only player to score a hat-trick in a World Cup final.

1950 - A World Cup to forget

England's first appearance at the World Cup was in 1950. It was a disastrous campaign for the Three Lions which included a shock 1-0 defeat to the amateurs of the USA.
